I have implemented the creator-side discovery feed ranking system for Aura. The feed now intelligently ranks creators based on their niche similarity to the logged-in user, ensuring the most relevant content appears first.

Key Implementation Details:
Similarity Scoring: I created the NicheMatcher utility to normalize tags and compute the overlap between the viewer and candidate creators.
Ranking Repository: The new CreatorRankingRepository handles fetching all candidate profiles, scoring them based on tag overlap, and sorting them with a multi-tiered priority:
Shared Tags: Creators with at least one shared tag always come first.
Overlap Count: More shared tags = higher rank.
Content Volume: Equal overlap is resolved by the creator's portfolioCount.
Recency: Tie-break based on the updatedAt timestamp.
Feed Fetching: PortfolioRepository was refactored to accept this ranked list, ensuring the vertical UI pages maintain the exact order computed by the ranking engine.
UI Integration: The VideoFeedViewModel now orchestrates the entire flow—from fetching your profile tags to ranking and loading the final content.
